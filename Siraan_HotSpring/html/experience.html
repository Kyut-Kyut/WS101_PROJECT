The Experience

Why Sira-an?

♨️

Natural Hot Springs

Soak in geothermally heated mineral pools built right along the rocky coastline, with water temperatures that soothe tired muscles and rejuvenate the spirit.

🌊

Oceanfront Pools

Multiple pools of varying temperatures are perched on the sea cliffs, offering unobstructed views and unforgettable sunset bathing.

🌅

Stunning Sunsets

The west-facing coastline delivers breathtaking golden-hour views every evening, turning the pools into mirrors of amber and coral light.

🏨

Comfortable Rooms

Air-conditioned accommodations with multiple bed configurations are available on-site for dawn-to-dusk enjoyment of the springs.

🏊‍♂️

Family-Friendly Fun

A large oceanfront pool with a water slide makes Sira-an fantastic for families and groups seeking both relaxation and adventure.

📍

Nearby Attractions

Use Sira-an as a gateway to Nogas Island, Punta Nasog headland, the Anini-y Baroque Church, and Mt. Aliw-Liw trekking trails.
